How to List a Treasure

Three easy steps

  1. Type the item's SKU in the box (for example, ANT-001). This is the same SKU you gave the item in Square.
  2. Pick where to sell it. All three marketplaces are already checked. Uncheck any you don't want — leave them all checked to list everywhere.
  3. Click "List This Treasure." Wait a moment for the message at the bottom.
A green ✓ means it worked. A red ✗ means something needs a look — usually the SKU was typed wrong or isn't in Square yet. Just fix it and click again.
